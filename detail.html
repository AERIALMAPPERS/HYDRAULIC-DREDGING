About Company


AM DATA LAB PVT LTD  is the  company who excavate the material from a water environment. Possible reasons for dredging include improving existing  water features; reshaping land and water features to alter  drainage,  navigability, and commercial use; constructing  dams,  dikes, and other controls for streams and shorelines; and recovering valuable mineral deposits or marine life having commercial value
